Our Mission

The Ecology Project is an ambitious initiative designed to bridge the gap between urban development and environmental preservation. By utilizing state-of-the-art Random Forest and XGBoost models, we analyze critical factors like Greenness, Urban Density, and Air Quality (PM 2.5) to provide actionable insights for city planners and environmentalists.

Data Driven
Analyzing millions of geo-spatial data points across urban grids.
AI Powered
Predictive modeling for future environmental scenarios.

Feature Importance

Our model identifies Greenness and PM 2.5 as the most significant predictors of overall urban health and sustainability.

Preserving Biodiversity

Forests are the lungs of our planet. Our models help identify areas where reforestation can have the maximum impact on air quality.

Sustainable Urbanism

Integrating nature into our cities isn't just a luxury—it's a necessity for a healthy future.

Water Security

Monitoring environmental health includes protecting our precious water resources from urban runoff and pollution.
